The History Of Our Hotel

Serenity Hotel began as a vision to create a peaceful retreat where guests could escape the hustle of everyday life.
Founded in 2024, nested in Nairobi, Kenya it was initially a small,family run inn known for its warmth and hospitality.
Over the years, it has evolved, blending luxury with tranquility, all while preserving its original charm.
with each renovation, we have committed to maintaining an eco-friendly approach, reflecting our respect for the surrounding environment and community
